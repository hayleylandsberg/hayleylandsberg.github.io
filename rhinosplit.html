RHINOSPLIT

A Digital Wallet

A UX/UI case study for a fictional digital wallet startup company geared towards millennials.

Problem Statement

Rhinosplit wants to provide a safer and easier way to transfer money between millennials. Also encourage millennial to be more responsible and pay back their friends.

Research Plan

What activities do you need to understand or observe?

  • Interaction between users
What environments will be important to visit your participants in?
  • N/A
What interactions between people, or object and person, do you need to understand or observe?
  • What pain points users have while dividing up bill at restaurant
What objects need exploring?
  • Making sure the site is secure
What types of users will you need to speak with and observe?
  • Millennials
  • People who transfer money from P2P
  • Possibility of B2B - version 2.0

Persona

A persona is a representation of a type of customer. Personas answer the question, “Who are we designing for?” and they help to align strategy and goals to specific user groups.

SWOT Analysis

Competitor evaluations for two of the top players currently in the market.

Interview Guide

Common questions asked during the interview process.

  • Which money transfer apps do you currently have accounts for (Venmo, PayPal, Zelle, etc)?
  • What made you start using the app?
  • What makes you use your choice app over others on the market?
  • Why do you use an app over other methods of payment (cash, credit card, etc)?
  • What scenario do you normally use the app in?
  • What do you like about the app?
  • What do you dislike about the app?
  • What features would you change and why?
